Let's Make a Picnic

A picnic is a time where we can get together on a sunny day. Though not everyday is sunny, sometimes it might be too cold or it might be rainy. Some people have no access to a nice peaceful park to settle in and enough a meal together in the open arms of nature. I welcome you to my simple simulator where you can imagine your own picnic. You can organize it however you'd like or use it for your future picnic and plan out your aesthetics for this picnic. Have your own picnic and enjoy your digital meal with friends and family.
